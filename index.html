A Bit About Me....

Introduction | An Endless Curiosity

I was born and raised in Upper Darby Pennsylvania and have lived here my entire life. I've always had an interest in technology, software specifically, and would always wonder what was going on behind the scenes of video games, computers, and websites.

One Video That Started It All

One night I was so curious I went to Google and typed how to make a game in 15 minutes because I was tired and lazy. It's actually pretty funny, after looking it up, I came across a video by that exact title, so naturally, I clicked on it. I wasn't expecting anything amazing to happen. I was so wrong, this moment was critical, as I ended up creating my first "game" in BATCH. At the end of the day, the program was just a simple quiz game, but it was really the start of an absolute obsession with BATCH and eventually programming in general. So thank you Ben Montoya , your video sparked my interest in programming, even if it was just a BATCH program. If it wasn't for this moment, I wouldn't have had that surging desire to become a developer, and I would have never learned other languages. it was the stepping stone to my endless curiosity as a young aspiring developer.

Keep Calm, I do In Fact Have A Social Life

I like to go out to eat when I can, and I'm obsessed with Applebees baby back ribs a little more than I care to explain. I also like to play video games, and I'm the proud owner of an original Playstation 2 that I've had since 2002 (she hasn't died on me yet!). When I'm not playing games, coding, or eating, I'm hanging out with my friends. Thanks so much for reading, have a great day! :) Oh... Also, scroll down the page, check out my projects! :D
